The Full Process

From certification to
on-chain proof.

Every CarbonBridge token (CBT) is traceable from its origin project to its final retirement. Here's how the entire lifecycle works.

Step 01 — Origin 🌿
Verified Carbon Projects

Carbon reduction or removal projects (forests, renewable energy, cookstoves, soil sequestration) are developed and independently audited by third-party verifiers. Projects must demonstrate additionality — the reductions would not have happened without the project.

01

What counts as a project?

  • 🌲 Avoided deforestation (REDD+)
  • ☀️ Renewable energy deployment
  • 🔥 Clean cookstove distribution
  • 🌊 Blue carbon (mangroves, seagrass)
  • 🌱 Soil carbon sequestration

Registry Certification

Projects are submitted to Gold Standard or Verra's Verified Carbon Standard. Rigorous methodology, monitoring and verification protocols must be met before any credit is issued.

Gold Standard Verra VCS
02
Step 02 — Certification 📋
Registry Approval & Credit Issuance

Once a project passes verification, the registry issues carbon credits — each representing exactly one tonne of CO₂ equivalent reduced or removed. These credits are recorded in the registry's ledger.

Step 03 — Tokenisation ⛓
Minting CBT Tokens on Blockchain

CarbonBridge acquires verified credits from the registry and immediately mints a corresponding CBT (CarbonBridge Token) on-chain. The originating registry credit is simultaneously marked as retired in the registry, preventing any double use.

03

On-chain data stored per token:

  • TOKEN_ID   ·   unique on-chain identifier
  • REGISTRY   ·   Gold Standard / Verra
  • PROJECT_ID · registry project reference
  • VINTAGE   ·   year of CO₂ reduction
  • METHODOLOGY · verification standard used
  • STATUS   ·   ISSUED / HELD / RETIRED

Purchase options:

  • 🔗 Connect Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect)
  • 🏢 Organisational login portal
  • 💳 Bank transfer for institutional buyers
  • 📊 API integration for automated purchasing
04
Step 04 — Purchase 🏢
Organisations Buy Tokens

Organisations calculate their carbon footprint and purchase the matching number of CBT tokens. Each token = 1 tonne CO₂e offset. Tokens are transferred to the organisation's wallet or portfolio account.

Step 05 — Retirement 🔥
Token Retirement (Burning)

When an organisation is ready to claim their offset, they initiate a retirement transaction. The tokens are sent to a provably unspendable burn address — permanently destroyed on-chain, with the transaction hash serving as an immutable retirement certificate.

05

Your retirement certificate includes:

  • TX_HASH · blockchain transaction hash
  • BLOCK · block number & timestamp
  • TOKENS_RETIRED · quantity & tCO₂e
  • RETIREE · organisation wallet address
  • BENEFIT_TO · offset beneficiary

Recognised Standards

Certified by the best.

GS

Gold Standard

The premium benchmark for climate and development. Founded by WWF. Requires projects to demonstrate measurable sustainable development co-benefits beyond carbon.

VCS

Verra Verified Carbon Standard

The world's most used voluntary carbon standard. Over 2,300 registered projects globally. Rigorous additionality, permanence and verification requirements.

CCP

ICVCM Core Carbon Principles

The Integrity Council for the Voluntary Carbon Market's global quality benchmark. CCP-eligible credits represent the highest integrity tier in the voluntary market.

When a CBT token is retired, it is sent to a provably unspendable burn address on the blockchain — a public address for which no private key exists or can ever exist. This is a cryptographic guarantee: it is mathematically impossible to move tokens out of this address. The blockchain's immutability means this record can never be altered. Additionally, the underlying registry credit (Gold Standard or Verra) is simultaneously cancelled, preventing any corresponding credit from entering the system again.
