The Problem

Carbon markets
need trust.

Traditional carbon credits suffer from opacity, double-counting, and fraud. Organisations spend millions on offsets they can't verify.

CarbonBridge solves this by tokenising certified carbon credits on a public blockchain — every token's lifecycle is immutably recorded from issuance to retirement.

The Process

From forest to ledger
in four steps.

01
🌿

Verified Projects

Carbon reduction projects are audited and certified by Gold Standard or Verra, generating verified credits.

02
⛓

Token Minting

Each verified tonne of CO₂ reduction is minted as a unique, non-fungible CBT token on-chain with full provenance.

03
🏢

Organisation Purchase

Organisations connect their wallet or use our portal to purchase the exact number of tokens matching their footprint.

04
🔥

Retirement & Proof

Tokens are publicly burned on-chain — creating an immutable, auditable retirement certificate for reporting.

Standards We Support

Every token backed
by the world's leading
carbon registries.

CarbonBridge tokens are minted exclusively against credits from Gold Standard and Verra's Verified Carbon Standard — two of the most rigorous and recognised certification frameworks globally.

This means every CBT you purchase represents a real, additional, measurable tonne of CO₂ equivalent reduced or removed.

Blockchain Integrity

Why blockchain changes everything.

🔒

Immutable Records

Every token issuance, transfer and retirement is permanently recorded on a public blockchain — no entity can alter or delete it.

♾️

No Double Counting

Each CBT token represents exactly one tonne. Once retired (burned), it cannot be reused, transferred, or claimed again — mathematically impossible.

🔍

Instant Auditability

Any regulator, auditor or stakeholder can verify your retirement proofs in seconds using our public explorer — no need to trust our word.

⚡

Real-Time Settlement

Purchases and retirements settle in minutes, not weeks. Eliminate the back-office friction of traditional carbon registries.
