Our Mission

Bridging the gap between
climate action and trust.

We built CarbonBridge because carbon markets desperately need a new infrastructure — one where claims can be verified by anyone, and where double-counting is impossible by design.

Why We Exist

The carbon market
has a trust problem.

"We believe every tonne of CO₂ offset should be verifiable by any child with an internet connection — not just auditors with access to private registries."

In 2023, investigations revealed that over 90% of some major registry's forest credits may have been worthless — so-called "phantom credits" that allowed corporations to claim carbon neutrality on paper while changing nothing in reality.

CarbonBridge was founded to fix this at the infrastructure level. By anchoring each carbon credit to a unique, non-reusable blockchain token, we make the entire offset lifecycle — from project certification to retirement — mathematically verifiable and permanently auditable.

The Problem

Three failures in
today's carbon markets.

01
Opacity & Trust Deficit

Registry ledgers are often private or difficult to access. Buyers have little way to independently verify that the credits they purchased are real, additional, and unretired elsewhere.

02
Double Counting Risk

Without a unified, public tracking system, the same underlying emission reduction can theoretically be claimed by multiple parties — undermining the entire premise of offsetting.

03
Friction & Inefficiency

Traditional processes involve brokers, weeks of settlement, paper certificates, and significant back-office burden — making offsets inaccessible for smaller organisations.
